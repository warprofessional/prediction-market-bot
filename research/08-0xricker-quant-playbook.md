## The 6 Formulas

### 1. LMSR: Price_i = e^(q_i/b) / Σe^(q_j/b)
- Edge: Daily $500+ on impact arb in volatile markets
- Risk: Thin pools (b<50), whale manipulation

### 2. Kelly: f* = (p*odds - (1-p)) / odds
- Use 0.25-0.5 fractional
- "Turns $1K into $150K over Q1 2026 with consistent +EV bets"

### 3. EV Gap: EV = (p_true - price) * payout
- **Entry ONLY if EV > 0.05 after fees** ← KEY: higher threshold than ours
- "$300+ daily on $2K bankroll scanning geo/politics"

### 4. KL-Divergence: D_KL(P||Q) = Σ P_i log(P_i/Q_i)
- Measures correlation mispricing between related markets
- Arb if KL > 0.2 threshold
- "15% portfolio uplift in diversified bets"

### 5. Bregman Projection: min D_φ(μ||θ) s.t. constraints
- Multi-outcome arb optimizer
- "$496 average per trade, near-zero downside"
- 50-150 convergence iterations

### 6. Bayesian Update: P(H|E) = P(E|H)*P(H)/P(E)
- "+12% accuracy in volatile geo/news markets"
